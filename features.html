By default, Slack encrypts data at rest and data in transit for all your communications. We offer a partner ecosystem with tools such as Slack Enterprise Key Management (EKM), audit logs and integrations with top data loss prevention (DLP) providers.

Slack offers governance and risk management capabilities flexible enough to meet your organization’s needs no matter where they are. This includes global retention policies, custom terms of service and support for eDiscovery.
